Claim Form

A Brief

This was a project I worked on during my internship. I was assigned to design and develop a digital claim form for internal staff, specifically under the HR Management team.

I was genuinely excited about this task — it had a real impact across the company. The design is clean and minimal, with a layout that makes navigation easy for users.

My Work

Before this system existed, staff had to manually fill out claim details using Excel sheets — definitely a time-consuming process.

I drew design inspiration from Pinterest and worked closely with the UI/UX developer to align the form with the company's style and workflow.

The form is also designed to integrate with the company’s internal web-based employee engagement system, which handles scheduling, payroll, communication, and performance feedback.
